I have been making 3D models since 2016 using Autodesk Maya and I specialize in creating low-poly stylized game ready 3D assets. I chose low-poly art because I love its stylized nature that can stand the tests of time, its quick turn around rate that allows a lot more models for projects and the ability to add extra details to models without heavily increasing poly count. I often describe my style as low-poly but high-detail!
